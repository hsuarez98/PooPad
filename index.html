How it works
POOPad – The first deflationary launchpad on the ShitCoins ecosystem
The main deflationary mechanisms of POOPad include a 10% fee on all token sells and early unstaking fees of up to 25%
Sell Fees distribution
  • 7.5% of the fees will be rewarded to stakers in flookiboy
  • 2.5% will be burned forever
Early unstaking Fees
  • There will be an option to unstake early which comes with an up to 25% burn fee. If you wait 8 weeks to unstake, there will be NO FEE
